Our Tumuaki (Principle)

Kia Ora Parents and Students,


Our principal, Mr Justin Harper, is a man who brings a deep sense of purpose, faith, and leadership to John Paul College. With a strong background in both business and education, Mr Harper began his teaching journey after completing his Commerce degree at Victoria University, followed by teacher training in Tauranga and a Master’s degree at the University of Waikato. He has taught in both state and Catholic schools and held senior leadership roles, including principal at Cullinane College in Whanganui. Since joining us in 2022, he has continued to uphold the Lasallian values that guide our school community: Through Faith and Love – Mā te Pono me te Aroha.


Mr Harper is passionate about creating a school environment where every student feels known, valued, and challenged to grow. He believes in the power of education to transform lives and is committed to maintaining high standards while nurturing a culture of care, respect, and belonging. Outside of school, Mr Harper is a dedicated family man who enjoys running, gardening, mountain biking, and coaching sports when time allows. We are proud to have him as the leader of our John Paul College family.
