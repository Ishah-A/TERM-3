Teachers, Staff and Faculty

At John Paul College we have a wide variety of teachers respective to their subject. We have deputy principles, heads of faculties and teachers in their subjects. The list down below can be used if you wish to contact one of the teachers or faculty members within our school for any reason - questions, emails or notifying them of your child's absence.


The teaching staff and faculty are committed to providing a high-quality, values-based education rooted in the Catholic tradition. Teachers at JPC are known for their supportive and approachable nature, fostering strong relationships with students while encouraging academic excellence, personal growth, and faith development. With a wide range of subjects on offer—from core academic disciplines to the arts, sports, and technology—teachers work collaboratively to deliver engaging and meaningful learning experiences that cater to all students' strengths and interests.


The faculty at this school includes passionate specialists across various departments, many of whom bring years of experience and dedication to their roles. Whether it’s the Deans who provide pastoral care and support, the Heads of Faculty who lead academic departments, or the Senior Leadership Team who guide the college’s strategic direction, each staff member plays an important part in nurturing a safe, inclusive, and vibrant school community. Their shared goal is to help students grow into confident, compassionate, and well-rounded individuals, ready to contribute positively to society.
