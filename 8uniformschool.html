FAQ's About The UNIFORM CODE

"AM I ALLOWED TO WEAR MY OWN JERSEYS/JACKETS TO SCHOOL?"
Yes you can wear them to school and after school just not during the school hours.

"CAN I WEAR GLOVES IN THE WINTER?"
Yes you can wear gloves, but only after school hours.

"HOW MANY EARRINGS AM I ALLOWED TO WEAR?"
You may have a maximum of two earrings in each ear - plain sleepers and studs only.

"HOW OFTEN DO I NEED TO SHAVE MY FACIAL HAIR?"
For boys it is compulsory to shave your faical hair regardless of how long it is.

"HOW LONG CAN MY HAIR BE?"
Your hair may be as long as shouler length before it must be tied up in a hairtie.

"AM I ALLOWED TO WEAR MAKEUP AT SCHOOL?"
No, you are not allowed to wear heavy amounts of makeup.

"CAN WE MIX AND MATCH THE UNIFORM WITH OUR OWN CLOTHES OR OTHER PARTS OF THE UNIFORM?"
You are not allowed to mix and match the school uniform with your personal clothing. Also, mixing and matching is not allowed say for exmaple with a winter shirt and summer pants.
