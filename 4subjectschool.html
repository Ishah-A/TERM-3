Subject Information

Subjects at John Paul College

There are plenty of different subjects for you to involve yourself in here at the school, there are many oportunities for you to be able to grow, thrive and find out what best suits you for your years ahead.





Mathematics

The Mathematics Faculty at John Paul College is dedicated to helping students develop strong problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and confidence in working with numbers. The teachers are approachable, knowledgeable, and supportive, offering clear explanations and plenty of guidance to help students succeed at all levels. From junior classes to senior NCEA courses, the faculty encourages students to challenge themselves and apply mathematical concepts to real-world situations.
We offer: Maths (MAT), Math with Statistics (MSA) and Math with Calculus (MAC)




Languages

The Languages Faculty at John Paul College offers students the opportunity to explore and appreciate different cultures through language learning. With passionate and supportive teachers, students can study languages such as Enlgish, Te Reo Māori and Spanish, developing communication skills and cultural understanding. The faculty encourages participation, celebrates diversity, and fosters a love for language through engaging lessons, cultural activities, and real-world applications.
We offer: English (ENA), Te Reo Maori (MAO), Te Ao Haka (TAH), Senior English Language (SEL) and Spanish (SPA).




Sciences

The Science Faculty at John Paul College inspires curiosity and a deeper understanding of the natural world through hands-on learning and inquiry-based teaching. Covering subjects like Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, the faculty provides a strong foundation in scientific principles while encouraging students to think critically and explore how science impacts everyday life. With enthusiastic and knowledgeable teachers, students are supported in both theoretical and practical work, preparing them well for future study and careers in science-related fields.
We offer: Science (SCI), General Science (GSC), Physics (PHY), Biology (BIO) and Chemistry (CHE).




Humanities

The Humanities Faculty at John Paul College encourages students to explore human society, history, culture, and the world around them. Subjects such as History, Geography, and Social Studies help students develop critical thinking, research skills, and a deeper understanding of global and local issues. The dedicated teachers in this faculty create engaging, discussion-based learning environments where students are encouraged to ask questions, form opinions, and make connections between the past and present.
We offer: Social Studies (SOS), History (HIS), Psychology (PSY), Classical Studies (CLS), Legal Studies (LGS) and Geography (GEO).




Technologies

The Technologies Faculty at John Paul College offers students a creative and practical approach to learning through subjects such as Digital Technology, Hard Materials and Food Technology. With a focus on problem-solving, innovation, and hands-on experience, students are encouraged to think critically and develop real-world skills. The teachers in this faculty are supportive and experienced, guiding students through projects that combine technical knowledge with creativity, preparing them for both future study and careers in a range of technological fields.
We offer: Digital Technologies (DGT), Product & Spatial Design (DVC), Food & Nutrition (FAN), Hopsitality (HOS), Matetial Technology (MET) and Wood-based Technology (WBT).




Arts

The Arts Faculty at John Paul College is a vibrant and creative space where students are encouraged to express themselves through a range of artistic disciplines, including Drama, Music, Dance, Visual Art, Design, and Photography. With passionate and skilled teachers, the faculty nurtures talent and creativity, offering students opportunities to perform, exhibit, and develop their artistic abilities. Whether it’s on stage, behind a camera, or through visual design, students are supported to explore their unique voices and refine their technical skills. The Arts Faculty plays a key role in enriching the school’s culture, with regular showcases, performances, and exhibitions that celebrate student creativity.
We offer: Fine Art (PAI), Photography (PHO), Design (DES), Drama (DRA), Music (MUS) and Dance (DAN.)




Religious Education

The Religious Education (R.E.) Faculty at John Paul College plays a central role in supporting the school’s Catholic character and values-based education. Through engaging lessons, students explore faith, ethics, social justice, and the teachings of the Catholic Church, while also being encouraged to reflect on their own beliefs and values. The R.E. teachers are compassionate and approachable, fostering a respectful and inclusive environment where students feel comfortable discussing spiritual and moral topics. This faculty also helps lead school liturgies, retreats, and service opportunities that strengthen the sense of community and connection at JPC.
We offer: Religious Education (RED) and Faith and Action (FIA).




Careers / Trades

The Careers and Trades department at John Paul College is dedicated to helping students plan and prepare for their futures beyond school. Whether students are aiming for university, polytechnic, apprenticeships, or the workforce, the department offers guidance, resources, and personalised support. Students can explore a range of pathways through career expos, workplace visits, and the Gateway programme, which provides hands-on experience in various trades and industries. The staff in this department work closely with students to help them discover their strengths, set goals, and make informed decisions about their next steps.
We offer: Careers (CAR) and Tourism (TOU)




Physical Education & Health

The Physical Education and Health Faculty at John Paul College promotes overall well-being by encouraging students to lead active, healthy lifestyles. Through a mix of practical physical activity and classroom-based learning, students develop skills in teamwork, resilience, and personal health awareness. The faculty offers a supportive and energetic environment, where students engage in a variety of sports, fitness activities, and health topics that are relevant to their everyday lives. Teachers are passionate about helping students build confidence, stay active, and make positive choices for their physical and mental well-being.
We offer: Health and P.E (HPE), Sports Science (SSC) and Activity Based Learning (ABL).




Economics

At John Paul College, Economics and related subjects help students understand how the world works — from personal finances to global markets. Whether you’re learning about supply and demand, budgeting, or how governments make economic decisions, these subjects give you real-life skills for the future. Economics, Accounting, and Business Studies are offered at senior levels and provide great pathways for careers in business, finance, and entrepreneurship.
We offer: Economics (ECO) and Business Studies (BUS).
