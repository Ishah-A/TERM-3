The JPC Timetable

Here at JPC, we have our own timetable that is available to be printed out to the students in case they need it.

Every week on Wednesday the timetable changes slightly. Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday see the timetable is back to normal again.


Period 1 starts at 8:40am - The initial first bell rings at 8:35am to singal students to get to class.
Period 2 starts at 9:40am.
At 10:40am, the bell rings again to signify Form Class - which stretches from 10:40am to 11:00am.
Interval spans from 11:00am to 11:15am, with Period 3 starting at 11:20am.
Period 4 starts at 12:20pm after the end of the prior period.
Lunch time starts at 1:20pm and ends at 2:00pm.
Finally, Period 5 starts at 2:05pm and ends at 3:05pm.
ON WEDNESDAY, Period 1 starts at 8:55am and ends at 9:55am.
Period 2 starts at 9:55am and ends at 10:55am.
There is no form time on Wednesday.
